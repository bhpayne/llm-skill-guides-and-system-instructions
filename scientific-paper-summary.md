Journal club for graduate students

- Identify the research question
- Is the paper testing a novel theory, refining a fundamental constant, or building a better instrument?
- what are the two or three most important claims or conclusions in this paper?
- Is the content theoretical, simulation, or experimental? 
- what are two or three key parameters (whether variable or constant) in this paper?
- what are the assumptions that constrain the scope for applicability?
- for Experiments, do the physical limits of the apparatus (e.g., diffraction limit, thermal noise, quantum noise) restrict the claims?
- What assumptions are made that weaken the results of this paper?
- What claims or assumptions, if any, are counter to consensus in the domain of this paper?
- is there open source software released in association with this paper?
- is the data used in this paper publicly available?
